Hello There! 👋

I have created this blog to be a space where I can share my thoughts, ideas, and creations with others. I have a passion for creating things through coding and I am constantly trying to learn more. I am very curious and I love to experiment with new ideas and projects. If you want to learn more about me and my story you can check out my 'About' page.

Some of the things that you can see on my website are:

  • Blog: This is where I occasionally will post articles on ideas I may have or tips & tricks I want to share.
  • My Setup: On my setup page you will be able to see my office setup including all my hardware and links to where I got it from. You can also find my computer setup here which includes all of my apps, packages, and extensions. Lastly, my preferred IDE and its extensions will be listed here as well.
  • My Projects: The projects that I am currently working on and ones that I have completed that I feature on this page.
  • About Me: Not everyone's journey is the same and I know that I have gained insight from reading other developer's stories so I thought it would be great to share with all of you how I got into developing and things I have learned along the way.
  • Contact: If you want to contact me, go to my Contact page and listed there are all the ways you can contact me!
