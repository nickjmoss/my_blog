Introduction

Hi! I am so glad that you are here on my website that I have built from scratch, without a website generator, using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript! This project started out super small back when I was in the beginning days of my Web Programming I class and my professor asked us to create a simple 'About Me' page with some very simple HTML and CSS.

Back then, I wouldn't have thought that I would be able to build my very own dynamic and responsive blog but after lots of learning, trial and error, and practice I have been able to create something that I am sincerely proud of.

Having the ability to literally bring your ideas to life is one of the reasons why I love programming so much and the satisfaction I get from finishing a project and having it be fully functional is out of this world. However, I never really was into programming.
